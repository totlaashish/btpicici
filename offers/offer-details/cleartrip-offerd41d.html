II: Offer:

Book any 4 Domestic Flights on Cleartrip with ICICI Bank Netbanking & get 1 FREE Flight Voucher worth Rs.5,000 delivered directly into your inbox!

  • Valid for first 4,000 Qualifiers who book any Domestic Flight on Cleartrip using ICICI Bank Netbanking.
  • Remember to use the same email id & Coupon CTICICINB across all your 4 transactions, to ensure you qualify faster!
  • To qualify for the FREE Flight Voucher, the total transaction value of your first 4 transactions has to be a minimum of Rs.25,000 only. That’s works out to Rs.6250 per transaction x 4 only.
  • Choose & book any leading Low Cost & Full-Service Airline in India for any travel datebefore 31 May 2019.
  • No Hidden T&C. No Blackout dates. Just book & fly for FREE. It's that Simple!

How to book & qualify for the Free Flight Voucher?

  • Visit www.cleartrip.com or download the Cleartrip Mobile App available for iOS on App Store & for Android on Google Play Store.
  • Book any Domestic One-way or Roundtrip Flight for any travel date before 31 May 2019.
  • Simply use the same email id & enter coupon code CTICICINB across all your 4 transactions.
  • Make payment with ICICI Bank Netbanking. Any part or full payment made with Cleartrip Wallet balance is not eligible.
  • To qualify for the FREE Flight Voucher, the total transaction value of your first 4 transactions has to be a minimum of Rs.25,000 only.
  • Valid for transactions made from 21 November 2018 to 31 May 2019.

How to redeem my FREE Flight Voucher?

  • If you are amongst first 4,000 Qualifying Customers, just sit back & relax!
  • Cleartrip will email a Voucher code to redeem 1 FREE Flight worth Rs.5,000, directly into your inbox.
  • The FREE Flight Voucher will emailed to the first 4,000 eligible ICICI Bank Netbanking users within 40 Days from their date of qualification.
  • The FREE Flight Voucher is valid across all leading Low Cost & Full Service Airlines in India available on Cleartrip.
  • Redeem your FREE Flight Voucher within 60 days ie upto 15 July 2019. Travel Dates are completely open.
  • The FREE Flight Voucher offer is applicable on the total fare displayed on the Flights search results page. Convenience Fees & additional services purchased (eg: Insurance, Meals, Baggage etc) are payable extra.
  • If the total cost of the Flight exceeds Rs.5,000, then simply pay the excess balance with ICICI Bank Netbanking.
  • Each ICICI Bank Netbanking user can redeem the FREE Flight Voucher only onceduring the complete promotion period.
