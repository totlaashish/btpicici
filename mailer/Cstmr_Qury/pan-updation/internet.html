Procedure to update your PAN
Internet Banking
  • Visit www.icicibank.com/nri
  • Select Internet Banking
  • Enter your User ID and Password to login
  • Click on Service Requests (link on left hand panel of logged-in section)
  • Click on Request for PAN Updation
  • Select Account number and enter the following details:
    • PAN Card Number
    • Date of Birth on PAN Card
    • Registered E-mail ID
Alternatively, you can send an email from your secured inbox (left-hand panel of the logged-in page).

Turn around time: 2 working days
Click here to login now.

Email

You may send an e-mail to nri@icicibank.com from your registered e-mail ID mentioning your ICICI Bank account number / customer ID and PAN.

Turn around time: 2 working days
Phone Banking
  • Call our 24-hour Customer Care
  • Authenticate yourself
  • Speak to our customer care executive and request for PAN updation

Turn around time: 2 working days

Please Note: Keep your Debit Card Number, PIN and Account Number handy during the call for the authetication process.

To update your PAN details you can also visit any of our branches in India.
