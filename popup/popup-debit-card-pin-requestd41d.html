Debit Card PIN Request:

In case you do not have a PIN, you can generate PIN for your ICICI Bank Debit card by following steps:

  • Through Phone Banking IVRS (Interactive Voice Response System):
    • After welcome message, Select Option 1 for “NRI Customers”
    • Again select option 1 for “Bank account”
    • System will ask to enter debit card and PIN details for authentication or press 1 for APIN generation
    • Select option 1 for APIN generation.
  • Internet Banking (Online PIN generation):
    • After login Click on Customer Service
    • Service Request option available at left side of the page
    • Click on More option available under Bank account Click on Generate debit card PIN online
    • Select the card and enter CVV number (available at back of the card - 3 digit)
    • Click on submit
  • You may also Generate the PIN using the quick link “MY CARD PIN” available on the right side of the page in the login section below “MY PROFILE”.
  • Physical form:
