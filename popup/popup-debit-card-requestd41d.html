Debit Card Request:

In case you do not have a Debit Card, you can place the request for a new debit car through any of the following options:

  • You can call our 24 hour customer care and select option 2. You can request our official to take a request for a debit card.
  • In case you can log into your internet banking account, can place the request for the Debit card through service request:
    • Click on "Customer Service", select "Service Requests" option
    • Click on "More" option Under "Bank Account"
    • Under "ATM/Debit Card Related", select "Reissue of Lost ATM/Debit Card".
  • If you are unable to place the request, you can chat with our officials using the below mentioned steps:
    • Click on "Customer Service"
    • Click on “May I help you”
    • Click on “Start Online Chat Now”.
