Procedure for availing Debit Card Facility

1.Contact your Branch.

2.If you don’t have saving or Current Account, first open your account.

3.Get the Debit Card Application form from the Branch, fill it up and submit it to your Branch.

4.You will receive your Insta Debit Card at the time of submission of the application form.

5.In case of named Debit Card, ,the card will be delivered to the customer’s residential address. Un-delivered cards will be re-directed to Branch where the Account is maintained.

6. Customer can generate the PIN through their Registered Mobile Number via Tollfree Number 18002336427or022-62242424.Alternatively, the PIN can be generated from ATMs of Dena Bank.

7.The customer has to activate the Insta Debit card after 24 hours from the receipt of the card by withdrawing cash from Any Bank’s ATM or through Point of Sale (POS) Terminal.


Customer can perform following types of transactions:-

1.Cash withdrawal

2.Balance Enquiry

3.Mini statement

4.Verified by VISA transaction/ RuPay Pay Secure
