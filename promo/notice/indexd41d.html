Generate your TDS Certificate online for NRO Savings / Fixed Deposit.

 

TDS Certificates for the period October to December 2014 will be send to your registered email id by February 15, 2015.

 

In case you do not receive your TDS Certificate click here to download your TDS Certificate (available for April 2012 onwards).

 

In case your email id is not registered, a physical copy of TDS Certificate will be dispatched to your communication address registered with the bank as per the dispatch schedule mentioned below.

 

You can also call our  24-hour Customer Care to update your email ID and receive a copy of your TDS Certificate on email.

 

Quarterly TDS Certificates are issued for NRO Savings and Fixed deposits only if:

  • Interest has been earned and TDS has been deducted on any of the NRO Savings Accounts and Fixed deposits in that quarter.
  • Pan number is updated in our bank records. To know more about PAN updation, Click here

 

Please note a consolidated TDS Certificate is issued for all accounts within the same Customer ID.
