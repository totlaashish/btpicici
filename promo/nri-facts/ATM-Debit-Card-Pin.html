In this edition of the NRI Facts Series,we would be discussing how you can generate your ATM/Debit Card PIN.
It is important to memorise your PIN, as you would require it to authenticate yourself.
Once you open any savings account with ICICI Bank and request for ATM/Debit Card, your ATM/Debit Card PIN is sent by ICICI Bank to your communication address registered with us.
For security reasons, you are advised to change the PIN generated by ICICI Bank at the earliest by visiting the nearest ATM or by using any of the below mentioned service channels.
In case you forget your PIN, you would need to generate it again.You can use the following service channels for the same:
Call our Customer Care numbers available at www.icicbank.com/nri
Authenticate yourself with your Debit Card number
Request for PIN generation
Turn Around Time: Immediate
  Note: Please keep your Debit Card handy during the call
 
Log on to Internet Banking with your user ID and password
Select Service Request
Select Request for Issue of PIN
Turn Around Time: 10 days
For any further queries or to generate your ATM/Debit Card PIN you can also visit any of our branches in India.
Hope you find this information useful.
