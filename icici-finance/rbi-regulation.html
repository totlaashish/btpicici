Latest RBI Regulations important to you

Master Circular on Non-Resident Ordinary Rupee (NRO) Account - July 01, 2013 > What's in it for you : This circular contains all important guidelines like permissible credits and debits, repatriation rules etc. pertaining NRO account. Go through the circular to understand your NRO account in detail.

Master Circular of instructions relating to deposits held in FCNR (B) Accounts - July 01, 2013 > What's in it for you : This circular contains important guidelines like payment of interest , joint holding etc. pertaining FCNR (B) account. Please go through the circular to understand your FCNR account in detail.

Deregulation of Interest Rates on Non-Resident (External) Rupee (NRE) Deposits November 29, 2013 > What's in it for you : This means you can now earn higher interest rates on your NRE FDs.

Transfer of Funds from NRO Account to NRE Account – May 07,2012 > What's in it for you : This circular states that ,subject to payment of applicable taxes, you can now transfer funds from your NRO account to any NRE account within an overall ceiling of US $ 1 million per financial year.

Master Circular on Remittance Facilities for Non-Resident Indians / Persons of Indian Origin / Foreign Nationals July 01, 2013 > What's in it for you : This circular contains all important guidelines pertaining remittances of different types of income like salary , income from rent or dividend, income earned on sale of property etc. Go through the circular to understand various guidelines pertaining remittance you can make.
