Important Points to remember:

  • Resident can be added as joint applicant only on ‘former or survivor’ basis.
  • If Mode of Operation (MOP) in name addition form is selected as 'Jointly', Internet banking and Debit Card facility shall not be available for all the holders of the account.
  • Existing Accounts should not be under Debit Freeze / Lien or dormant status.
  • If existing Account has I Direct facility the name addition cannot be done.
